---
title: "Delete an Image"
---

To delete an image from a Roboflow project:

1. 1.  
Click on the image you want to delete on the Dataset tab associated with your project.

2. 2.  
Click the three dots in the top right corner of the page.

3. 3.  
Click "Remove From Project".

4. 4.  
Click "Confirm" in the confirmation pop up.

![](/images/datasets/Screenshot%202023-07-05%20at%2011.59.59.png)

Select "Remove From Project" to remove an image from a project.

After you click "Confirm", your image and any associated annotations will be permanently deleted.
