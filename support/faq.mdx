## How can I change my password?

Currently, the way to change your password is through the "reset your password" user flow.

1. 1.  
Sign out

2. 2.  
Sign in with email only  
   * Click the "trouble signing in" link

3. 3.  
Check email sent by Roboflow  
   * Click link to reset password  
   * Enter new password

## How do I change my workspace from private to public?

You can purchase our monthly Starter Plan directly in the app to make your workspace images and models private. If you are working on an academic project, you can also [apply for private images and research credits](https://roboflow.com/contribute).
