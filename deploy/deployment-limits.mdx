---
title: "Deployment Limits"
---

The table below shows device limits for Roboflow deployment options. If you are on an enterprise plan, contact your Roboflow representative for more information on your device limits.

| Inference Limits / Plan                     | Free      | Starter | Enterprise |
| ------------------------------------------- | --------- | ------- | ---------- |
| Hosted Inference (monthly API calls)        | 1,000     | 10,000  | Custom     |
| roboflow.js Sessions (monthly active users) | 1,000     | Custom  | Custom     |
| Inference Server Deployments                | 5 devices | Custom  | Custom     |
| OAK                                         | 5 devices | Custom  | Custom     |
| iOS (devices)                               | 1,000     | Custom  | Custom     |
