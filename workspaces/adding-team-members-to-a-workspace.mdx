---
title: "Add Team Members"
---

To invite a team member to a Workspace, add their email address in the Workspace Members section of the workspaces settings.

![](/images/workspaces/Screen%20Shot%202021-08-04%20at%2011.51.03%20AM.png)

When adding a new team member, choose between `Admin` (full access) or `Labeler` (Job only access). Labelers will only be able to see and annotate images assigned to them.

![](/images/workspaces/Screen%20Shot%202023-02-01%20at%2012.31.00%20PM.png)
